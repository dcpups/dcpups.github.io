DEFCON Furs - Furry Village

(Suite hours, events, and details are still being finalized and are subject to change)

This year DEFCON Furs will have our own "unofficial" village!

Located in the Jubilee Tower at Bally's Las Vegas and open daily during the convention, the DEFCON Furs - Furry Village is our official outpost at DEF CON 27.

The village will be providing a low-key, relaxed environment for everyone to come work on our badge challenge, work on other contests,
get away from the crowds between DEF CON talks or villages, or just relax and socialize with a drink.
We will also be taking donations for swag in the Furry Village, and swag pickup will be available for those who donate before the con.

Location: Jubilee Tower, Bally's Las Vegas (Suite number TBD)

Hours:

  • Thursday Aug 8th: 11:00 AM - 5:00 PM
  • Friday Aug 9th: 11:00 AM - 5:00 PM
  • Saturday Aug 10th: 11:00 AM - 5:00 PM
  • Sunday Aug 11th: 11:00 AM - 5:00 PM
