DEFCON Pups

Hackers who bark! DEFCON Pups is a social group of the pup and handler scene within the larger DEFCON Furs community. Specific pup events including in-hood meetups and moshes are offered during the DEFCON Furs larger events.

All events [except the swim] are in the DC Furs space and badges will be required. Cash Bar will be avalible, but please remove hoods before approching

Pup and Handler Social

Friday, Aug 9 - 3.00pm till 4.00pm - DEFCON Furs Lounge @ the Alexis Park

Come say hi, meet other Pups and Handlers, and share a bark or two. Come early for the QC Furries & Kinksters Meetup, stay late for the Beyond Binaries Mixer.

Pups 101

Saturday, Aug 10 - 2.00pm till 3.00pm - DEFCON Furs Lounge @ the Alexis Park

New to the pup and handler space? Want to learn what human pup play means to different people? Come join us for a chat and to hear different perspectives from different people involved in the pah (pups and handler) space.

Pups and Handlers Swim

Sunday, Aug 11 - 1.30pm till 2.30pm(or longer) - Alexis Park Pool TBD

Following the closing ceromony, come join use for a doggy paddle around at the pool in the Alexis Park. Hoods encouraged. Even if you dont want to swim, its a chance to reconnect with folks before the conference fully ends
